Service

Features QuadLabs offers

Individualized and Customized

QuadLabs will create and publish a mobile application on behalf of your high school and will sit down and customize all parts of the application, including adding new features as per your school's needs.

Total Control

Our apps are entirely controlled through one easy-to-use google spreadsheet allowing you to add and edit events, daily announcements, club information, student life posts and general updates.

Continous Support

QuadLabs will provide continous support to ensure your school's mobile application is always running and we address any issues that come up.

Low Pricing

For the first 3 months of operation, the service is completely FREE. If your school would like to go forward with the service, we charge just $1 for every 50 users on a monthly basis. For every dollar in profit, we will donate 50 cents to a nonprofit organization advancing educational accessibility.

The Process

Learn about the steps to get your school's app published!

Introductory Meeting
Schedule an introductory meeting where we will explain the services we offer and our pricing. We will discuss your school's needs, discuss features and general design for your application.
Design and Feature Finalization
Following the introductory meeting, we will present a final mock-up for how we envision your school's app, including requested features, design and customizations such as colors. Here we will finalize the exact plan and workflow.
Build and Publication
Our team will diligently work on designing and building your school's application. We will publish the application on your school's behalf on the App Store. Process takes between 4-6 months, including the app publication and approval time.
Beta Roll Out
We will work with you student government and school administration to do a beta roll out with about 50 participants. This would be to familiarize the school with the application and to weed out any possible software bugs. This beta testing period should last 1-2 weeks.
School-wide Roll Out
After a thorough beta testing period, we will work with your school to devise a plan to publicize and roll the application out, which may inlude creating marketing materials. Once everything is completely finalized, we will formally release the app on the AppStore and BOOM your school's application is up and running!
